Location

Swat Valley is situated in the north eastern tip of the Khyber Pukhtunkhawa province surrounded by the scenic valleys of Dir, Chitral and Gilgit. Saidu Sharif the capital of Swat is linked by metalled road with Islamabad/Rawalpindi and Peshawar via Mardan and Malakand Pass.
